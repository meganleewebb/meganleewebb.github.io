Wandering Time

Bicycle Polo, Perth

12 Sep 2011 11:34

Been doing some rides about Perth, and generally lazying about.

Yesterday decided to go check out the bike polo games. Got the

information from http://www.bikepolo.com.au/cities/perth/ and rocked

up. The game they play is "Hardcourt Bike Polo" or "Urban Bike Polo".

Check out the rules

http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cycle_polo#Hardcourt_bike_polo

Most of the bikes were fixed gear or single speed. I used my bike - after taking off the mirror and lowering the bar ends.

It was heaps of fun. Going to have to go back an play some more.
